About us

At Chopped Barbershop, we take pride in our services. Our attention to detail is what separates us from other barbershops. We strive for excellence in everything we do, never compromising quality for speed. In this line of business we meet new people everyday and building a connection with you is just as important as making sure you’ll receive a cut that you love. It is our passion helping clients achieve their personal style by perfectly tailoring our standard of service to your needs and vision. Whether you’re looking to get a clean fade or to indulge yourself in a relaxing hot towel shave, we ensure clients always walk out our door looking and feeling their best.

After one visit to Chopped, it’ll be the only place you’ll want to come to for all of your needs.
